FAQ's

What is Tech Odyssey?

Tech Odyssey is the annual technical fest organized by the Computer Society of India at K.R. Mangalam University. It provides a platform for upcoming tech enthusiasts to showcase their skills and compete with their peers in various events such as web development, competitive coding, e-sports, treasure hunt, quiz, and more.

Who can participate in Tech Odyssey?

Tech Odyssey is open to all tech enthusiasts, including students (Class 12th and above), professionals, and hobbyists. Participants can register for various events based on their interests and skills.

How can I register for Tech Odyssey?

You can register for Tech Odyssey by visiting our website and following the registration instructions provided. Registration fees are applicable and may vary for certain events.

What events are included in Tech Odyssey?

Tech Odyssey includes various events such as web development, competitive coding, e-sports, treasure hunt, quiz, and more exciting activities designed to create a memorable experience for all participants.

How can I get involved in Tech Odyssey?

There are various ways to get involved in Tech Odyssey, including participating in events, volunteering, or becoming a sponsor. You can also follow us on social media for updates and announcements.
