FAQ's

Who is eligible to enter the competition?

Tech Odyssey is open to all tech enthusiasts, including students (Class 12th and above), professionals, and hobbyists. Participants can register for various events based on their interests and skills.

What happens if someone cheats during the competition?

Any form of cheating will result in immediate disqualification. We uphold fair play and sportsmanship at all times. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

How are prizes awarded in the competition?

Prizes will be granted to the top-performing teams or individuals as determined by the judging panel. Additionally, all participants will receive a participation certificate.

What is the expected code of conduct for participants?

Participants are expected to maintain professionalism and respect towards their fellow competitors throughout the competition.

Can I participate in the Battle Blitz competition alone or do I need to be in a group?

The Battle Blitz competition requires participants to form teams. Each team must consist of a minimum of 4 members for BGMI and Free Fire and 5 members for Valorant.

Have more questions? Contact us at support@techodyssey.dev for assistance.
